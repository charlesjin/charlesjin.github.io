Bio and Interests

I am a first-year graduate student at MIT advised by Martin Rinard (link). I am broadly interested in robust machine learning and program synthesis.

Previously, I was a Research Engineer at Reservoir Labs (link). My research involved building compiler backends for task-based parallelism and heterogenous runtimes.

I graduated from Yale University in 2016 with a Joint B.S./M.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Mathematics.
