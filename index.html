Bio and Interests

I am a Research Engineer at Reservoir Labs (link). My current research involves building compiler backends for task-based parallelism and heterogenous runtimes. I am generally interested in applications of high-performance computing and developing the programming languages and abstractions involved in those efforts.

I graduated from Yale University in 2016 with a Joint B.S./M.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Mathematics, and am actively looking for graduate school opportunities to start in Fall 2019.
